The adventure of Brian and Chelsy begins over a decade ago, as members of the Divine Shepherd Youth Group.

Chelsy saw how manly and humorous Brian was while they went on mission trips to Colorado.

After Chelsy moved to Norfolk, the duo periodically kept in touch over the next twelve years. She secretly knew she had met her true love.

Brian was incredibly lucky to reconnect with Chelsy on a first date at the Berry and Rye in Omaha.

On a picnic for Chelsy's Birthday, Brian was instructing Chelsy on the finer points of skipping rocks. Chelsy misfired and nailed Brian right in the face.

He secretly knew he had met his true love.
