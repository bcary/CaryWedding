Our Story

They Met

In 2005, Chelsy and Brian met when they were placed in the same van for a mission trip to Colorado. Before they had even left the state, they had to be separated because they were fighting so much and Chelsy had thrown gummy bears at Brian. Love at first sight?

They Fell In Love

Being the sweet man he is, Brian planned a picnic for Chelsy's 25th birthday (Bird Day). They decided to skip rocks, which Chelsy had no idea how to do. She threw it like a frisbee and hit Brian in the nose. Struck by love!

They Got Engaged

Chelsy had been given a jar for Christmas with 90 reasons why Brian loved her, with the last one taped to the bottom. A week after the 90th note, Brian successfully surprised Chelsy while teeing up a golf ball on the range at the Links in Lincoln. She looked down to find him on one knee with a ring box in his hand!
